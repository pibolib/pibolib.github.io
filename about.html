Jonathan "pibolib" Ouf

Hello!

I'm a solo game developer from the Eastern United States.

I was born on October 17, 2003, which makes me 16 years old. I've been developing games in GameMaker for about 2 years, and I've recently switched to Godot Engine. I tend to develop games on my own because of a personal goal of complete consistency between game elements (i.e. Music, Art, and Gameplay.) Only a small fraction of games on this website have been developed with another person.

Outside of game development, I've been learning python for making tools and other software, and this website is one of my first experiments in the HTML/CSS/JS sphere.

I'm an avid fan of puzzle games, rhythm games, rpgs, and various other genres of games, and elements of those games end up finding their way into my own.

I've been learning Japanese for almost a year now, so you might see a small bit of Japanese influence here and there, whether in my games or in parts of other things. (Kinda a weeb, just a little.) This website will probably get a Japanese version, in the distant future.
