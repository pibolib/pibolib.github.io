Cubika is a puzzle game about making squares.


-Overview-


At its most basic level, Cubika is a game about making squares. You can make squares to get a high score, defeat enemies, clear objectives, and much more.

There are many modes for you to play through, each with its own goals and gameplay differences.

  • Aim for a high score in ENDLESS mode.
  • Clear set boards in PUZZLE mode.
  • Fight against enemies and explore a labyrinth in DUNGEON mode.
  • Fight against other players (locally) in BATTLE mode.
  • Try to get the highest score in 3 minutes in SCORE ATTACK mode.

-How to Play-

You start with a board filled with pieces. Your goal is to maximize the amount of points you can receive from clearing them. If you make a square out of the pieces of the same color by moving them together, you can trigger the square to clear it.

If you clear many squares in a row without moving pieces, a combo will start, increasing the amount of points you get per square. Also, you can make larger squares that give a larger base. Aim for a combination of both to maximize your score.

If you clear all pieces of a certain color, or all pieces in general, you will be rewarded with many more points.



Every few seconds, a new row of pieces will fall from the sky. If you attempt to clear something that cannot be cleared, a new row of pieces might be forced upon you. As you keep playing, pieces will come faster and faster. If a piece cannot land on the 10x10 grid, then it's game over.

Keep your board clean and keep your combos and clears large, and a high score will soon be yours.
